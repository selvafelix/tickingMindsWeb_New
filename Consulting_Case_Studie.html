Testing services for a leading loyalty management solutions provider

Test Tools optimization consulting for a leading loyalty management solutions provider

      Client had big plans for the launch of their flagship product which was undergoing re-engineering from a technology stack perspective and had strategies in place for wide variety of testing, but wanted to be sure about the tool selection for automating different types of prime tests - automation, performance, test data- as well as make appropriate recommendation on toolkit factoring for technology support and future product road maps. We understood the overall testing needs of the client, logical architecture of the portal, associated technology stack and analyzed existing tools to explore potential for reuse of tools for some of the tests. Recommended an apt mix of tools highlighting two options- open source and commercial tools- laid out SWOT analysis of them for easy decision making to choose the tools to be part of the overall automation tool kit.
