EXPLAINING EXPANSION TO UK/EUROPE

Expansion into Europe

    Ticking Minds has a long and successful track record in providing digital systems assurance services comprising multiple aspects of test strategy, planning and execution. In collaboration with new Core Team members Richard Cawdell, Steve Randall and John Brown, the firm is now expanding to cover UK and European markets from a base in London.


    Working from this new European centre and using local expertise to complement the existing team in Chennai, Ticking Minds will offer the full range of services that it has become known for


•  Providing strategic guidance early in the cycle, improving overall outcomes

•  Complementing in-house resources in established businesses

•  Bringing scarce and specialist skills and technology to bear in high growth businesses

•  Creating and managing test data

•  Bringing the power of automation to bear

•  Accelerating the test process to improve go-to-market and go-live.
